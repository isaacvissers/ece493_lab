# Research: Conference Registration for Authorized Users

## Decision: Payment requirement

**Decision**: Payment is required unless the price is set to 0.
**Rationale**: Supports paid events while allowing free registration flows.
**Alternatives considered**: Always free; always paid.

## Decision: Eligibility rules

**Decision**: Any authenticated user can register; no eligibility restrictions.
**Rationale**: Simplifies access and aligns with clarified requirement.
**Alternatives considered**: Role-based or invite-based eligibility.

## Decision: Registration options

**Decision**: No ticket/workshop/meal options; collect only required registration details.
**Rationale**: Keeps registration minimal and avoids optional complexity.
**Alternatives considered**: Ticket types only; ticket + workshop/meal selections.

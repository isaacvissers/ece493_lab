# Research: Assign Referees to Submitted Papers

## Decision 1: Referee count requirement
- **Decision**: Exactly three referees are required per paper.
- **Rationale**: Clarified requirement; enforces consistent assignment load and avoids partial reviews.
- **Alternatives considered**:
  - No maximum or flexible count (rejected: clarified as fixed number).
  - Range-based count (rejected: clarified as exactly three).

## Decision 2: Notification requirement
- **Decision**: Notifications are required and must be sent to assigned referees.
- **Rationale**: Clarification explicitly requires notifications as part of assignment.
- **Alternatives considered**:
  - Optional notifications (rejected: clarification mandates sending).
  - No notifications (rejected: clarification mandates sending).

## Decision 3: Role authorization
- **Decision**: Only users with the Editor role can assign referees.
- **Rationale**: Matches UC-13 primary actor and clarification.
- **Alternatives considered**:
  - Allow chairs or other staff (rejected: out of scope per clarification).

## Decision 4: Handling incorrect referee count
- **Decision**: Block submission if the provided referee count is not exactly three.
- **Rationale**: Clarified behavior; ensures data consistency.
- **Alternatives considered**:
  - Auto-trim or accept extras (rejected: conflicts with clarified rule).
